Where Time Slows and Stories Begin...


Step inside and you’ll find yourself wrapped in a space that feels timeless — wooden shelves lined with books, brick walls that echo stories, and cozy corners that invite you to linger a little longer. Everything about Books & Bricks is designed to feel like a pause in the middle of a fast-moving world — a place where you can sip slowly, read freely, and reconnect.

Our Roots

Born out of a love for literature, food, and old-school café culture, Books & Bricks blends the warmth of a vintage reading room with the soul of a classic American diner. Our menu is a tribute to comfort — with generous portions, familiar flavors, and thoughtful twists that keep you coming back.

A Space That Belongs to You

We’re proud to have fostered a growing community that finds inspiration, connection, and even quiet solitude here. From university students scribbling in notebooks to families sharing weekend breakfasts, Books & Bricks belongs to everyone who walks through our doors.

Whether you’re dropping in for your daily brew, diving into a book over pancakes, or celebrating small joys with friends — our café is your space to feel at home.

Come as you are. Leave a little fuller — in heart, in spirit, and in appetite.



The Team Behind the Magic

Meet the hearts and minds crafting every cup, every plate, and every smile at Books & Bricks.

Anam Khan

The heartbeat of daily operations, Anam makes sure everything at Books & Bricks runs smoothly — from the first pour of coffee in the morning to the last table served at night. With a passion for people and a natural flair for hospitality, she thrives on meeting new faces and building lasting connections. You’ll often find her chatting with guests, making sure everyone feels right at home.

Danish Bhat

Danish’s journey with hospitality began far from Srinagar — in the bustling kitchens of London. He returned home with a vision to build something meaningful for his community. Books & Bricks is a reflection of that dream — a space that bridges global experiences with local warmth.

Krushnaa Patil

Mountaineer turned culinary lead, Krushnaa quite literally knows how to handle the heat. (Yes, she’s climbed Mount Everest!) Today, she channels that same spirit of adventure into leading the kitchen at Books & Bricks. With an eye for detail and bold creativity, she ensures every dish is rooted in comfort — with a twist.

Srinagar’s Most Loved Café-Diner

Menu

Nestled in the heart of Srinagar, Books & Bricks Café is more than just a café — it’s an experience. A warm, rustic haven for book lovers, coffee seekers, and curious wanderers, our space invites you to slow down and savor the good things in life.

Whether you're here for a hearty American-style brunch, a quiet reading nook, or vibrant conversations over coffee, you’ll find your place with us.

Established in 2015, Books & Bricks Café was born from a simple idea —

To create a space that feels like HOME

A place where stories are shared over steaming mugs and hearty meals. What began as a quiet corner café has now become one of Srinagar’s most iconic destinations — a cultural hub for artists, students, tourists, and thinkers alike.

Where Comfort Meets Culture

Tucked away in the heart of Gogjibagh, Srinagar, Books & Bricks Café is a warm and welcoming hideaway where rustic charm meets soulful conversation.

Since opening our doors in 2015, we’ve grown into more than just a café — we’ve become a gathering place for thinkers, artists, students, travelers, and old friends.

Our Philosophy

We believe in slow living, real conversations, and comfort food that feeds the soul.

Our menu is inspired by classic American diner fare, reinvented with seasonal twists and local ingredients.

Each dish is prepared with care, because we know that good food brings people together.
